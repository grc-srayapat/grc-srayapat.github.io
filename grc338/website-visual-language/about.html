Welcome to Second Hand Score, a haven for fashion enthusiasts seeking a unique blend of curated vintage clothing bundles and bales. At our store, we believe that fashion is a timeless journey, and our curated collections reflect the rich tapestry of styles that have shaped decades of iconic looks. As pioneers in sustainable fashion, we offer a diverse selection of high-quality vintage clothing, sold by the pound in carefully crafted bundles and bales.

Our mission at Second Hand Score is rooted in the principles of sustainable style. By offering second-hand treasures, we not only celebrate the unique styles of the past but also contribute to a more eco-friendly future. Our team of fashion aficionados is dedicated to curating collections that tell a story and add a touch of history to your wardrobe. From vintage band tees to retro leather jackets, each item we offer is a carefully chosen piece that embodies the charm and character of bygone eras.

What sets us apart is our commitment to affordable fashion. We believe that everyone deserves to express their individuality through style without breaking the bank. At Second Hand Score, our pound-by-pound pricing allows you to score incredible vintage finds at unbeatable prices. Our inventory is always changing, ensuring that each visit to our store unveils new treasures. Explore our changing selections regularly to stay on top of the latest trends and discover unique pieces that resonate with your personal style.

Join us in the sustainable fashion movement at Second Hand Score, where each piece is a score waiting to be discovered, cherished, and embraced. Fashion is timeless, and so are you. Step into our store and find your perfect style in the world of vintage treasures.
